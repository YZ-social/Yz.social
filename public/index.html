About

Yz.social ("wise social")

  • Report immediate concerns to the public by tapping their location on the map.
  • These locations are shared over anonymous p2p with other users in your area.
  • Reported concerns will fade away over 10 minutes.

A mirror of this app can be run by anyone, in case this site is taken down.

YZ.social is building a totally free and open source, fully secure, peer-to-peer network for a new class of applications. The YZ network has no servers, no central database, no single point of failure. It is a true, fully decentralized network constructed, controlled and owned by its users. YZ Alert (Wise Alert) is the first application built on the YZ network.
